Our Mumbai

Mumbai (formerly called Bombay) is a densely populated city on India’s west coast. A financial center, it's India's largest city. On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic Gateway of India stone arch, built by the British Raj in 1924. Offshore, nearby Elephanta Island holds ancient cave temples dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva. The city's also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.

LOCAL TRANSPORT.

Photograph the city's unique transport modes, including the iconic black and yellow taxis, local trains, and the famous Mumbai Rickshaw.

MUMBAI'S SKYLINE.

Photograph the cityscape during different times of the day – sunrise, sunset, and the glittering city lights at night.

NATURE IN THE CITY.

Explore green spaces like Sanjay Gandhi National Park, Hanging Gardens, and Marine Lines for a contrast to the Urban Landscape.
